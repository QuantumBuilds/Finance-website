Poem: "The Oath"

I promise not to waste the wealth I hold,
To give to those whose stories remain untold.
I swear to share, and never be blind,
To help those who are left behind.

For wealth is but a fleeting thing,
It is the kindness that I bring,
That will echo long after I'm gone,
Helping others carry on.

So with this oath, I do declare,
That I'll be kind and always care.
